Privacy Policy

Effective 25 April 2026

1. Who we are

Customy Studio (“we”, “us”) is a remote marketing & AI studio. Reach us at customyagency@gmail.com or on WhatsApp +62 857-8506-5652.

2. What data we collect

Two things happen on this site:

  • Always-on (necessary): a single localStorage entry for your theme preference and your cookie-consent choice. This never leaves your device.
  • Only with your consent (you choose in the cookie banner): page views and scroll depth via Google Analytics 4; anonymised heatmaps and short session replays via Microsoft Clarity; ad attribution via Meta Pixel and TikTok Pixel if you arrived from a paid campaign of ours.

You can change or revoke your consent at any time by clearing the customy.consent.v1 entry in your browser's site data, or by emailing us. If you reject everything, no third-party scripts ever load.

If you contact us via the email or WhatsApp links on this site, we receive only the information you choose to send (your email address, your business name, your message).

3. How we use that data

  • To reply to your message and discuss a potential project.
  • If we work together, to deliver and operate the system we build for you.
  • To understand which content on this page actually helps prospects, and to improve it.
  • To measure the result of paid ads we run (only if you accept the marketing category).

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing.

4. Where data is stored

Email correspondence sits in our Google Workspace inbox. WhatsApp messages live in WhatsApp’s end-to-end encrypted infrastructure. Any project-specific data lives in tools we agree on with you (typically Notion, our shared dashboards, your own systems).

5. How long we keep it

Email and WhatsApp threads: kept indefinitely unless you ask us to delete them. Project data: kept for the duration of the engagement plus 12 months for reference, unless you ask us to delete sooner.

6. Your rights (GDPR + similar)

You can ask us at any time to:

  • Tell you exactly what data we hold about you.
  • Correct or update it.
  • Delete it (right to be forgotten).
  • Export it in a portable format.

Email customyagency@gmail.com with the subject line “Privacy request” — we reply within 30 days.

7. Third-party services on this page

Always loaded (necessary for the page to render): fonts from Google Fonts and Adobe Fonts (Typekit). Partner logos via Google’s favicon service. These can technically log your IP when you load the page.

Loaded only after your consent:

  • Google Analytics 4 — aggregate page views, traffic sources, basic engagement. IP anonymised.
  • Microsoft Clarity — anonymised heatmaps and short session replays. Auto-masks input fields.
  • Meta Pixel (Facebook / Instagram) — conversion tracking for paid campaigns we run.
  • TikTok Pixel — same purpose for TikTok campaigns.

You can opt out per category in the cookie banner. None of these are loaded unless you opt in.

8. Updates to this policy

If we change anything material, we’ll update the date at the top of this page. The current version is always the one you’re reading.
